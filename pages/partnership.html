C1.1: Company exhibitor (small promotion package)

Package includes the provision of one (1) small roll-up stand with the company's logo on every satellite GeNeDis event scheduled to happen during the annual sponsorship period, inclusion of the company's logo on the GeNeDis web-site and complimentary use of the GeNeDis sponsoring affiliation for the company's products.

C1.2: Company exhibitor (medium promotion package)

Package includes the provision of two (2) regular roll-up stands with the company's logo on every satellite GeNeDis event scheduled to happen during the annual sponsorship period, inclusion of the company's logo on the GeNeDis web-site, complimentary use of the GeNeDis sponsoring affiliation for the company's products, invitation of a company's representative to attend one (1) GeNeDis satellite event during the annual sponsorship period, and allowance to exhibit the company's products during one (1) GeNeDis satellite event in a booth of predefined size.

C1.3: Company exhibitor (large promotion package)

Package includes the provision of two (2) regular roll-up stands and a medium-sized banner with the company's logo on every satellite GeNeDis event scheduled to happen during the annual sponsorship period, inclusion of the company's logo on the GeNeDis web-site, complimentary use of the GeNeDis sponsoring affiliation for the company's products, invitation of a company's representative to attend two (2) GeNeDis satellite events during the annual sponsorship period, and allowance to exhibit the company's products during one (1) GeNeDis satellite event within a predefined booth.
